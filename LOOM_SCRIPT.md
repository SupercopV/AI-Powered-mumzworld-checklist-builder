## Show the Result

“What matters here is that the output is personalized, budget-aware, and multilingual. This helps a mom move from uncertainty into confident shopping.”

## Why It Matters

“I picked this problem because it happens before product comparison. If Mumzworld helps users answer ‘what should I buy for my situation?’, it can improve confidence, product discovery, and downstream conversion.”

## Measurement

“If I shipped this, the first metric I’d watch is checklist completion rate. Then I’d test it on 5% of eligible traffic and measure whether checklist users click into products and add items to cart more than the control group.”
